RMK SMILE FOUNDATION - ORPHANAGE VISIT

On the 21st of December 2024, the RMK Smile Foundation had the incredible opportunity to spend time at the Royal Promise Orphanage at Nkanfoa - Cape Coast where we were received and greeted with smiles, laughter and warmth hearts filled with joy. Our visit was a memorable and remarkable one since the day marked the first outreach the Foundation embarked after its establishment. We donated a few items such as rice, oil, drinks, fruits, water, and a few others. Most importantly is the time and attention we spent with the kids through bible studies, worship and dance, and fun games. These moments remind us of the impact we can have when we come together to uplift others. Thank you to everyone who contributed to making this visit possible. Just as Christ loved us so as we have to love one another.
