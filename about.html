ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

RMK Smile Foundation is a non-governmental, non-profit organization dedicated to bringing hope, support, and opportunities to underserved communities. Founded in June 2024, the foundation is the brainchild of Miss Rith Marie Kowfie, whose passion for humanitarian service and community development inspired the creation of a platform that brings smiles to the faces of the less privileged. Built on compassion, integrity, and a deep commitment to social impact, RMK Smile Foundation focuses on supporting vulnerable children, struggling schools, and marginalized communities across Ghana. We believe every person regardless of background deserves access to essential needs, quality education, and a fair chance to thrive. We exist to restore hope, uplift communities, and ensure every child and family has a reason to smile. With every project we undertake, we aim to break cycles of poverty and create lasting change.

Vision

To create a society where everyone has access to essential needs, quality education, and equal opportunities for growth and development.

Mission

To empower marginalized communities and schools by providing access to quality education, health support and equal opportunities.

Founder's Message

As the founder of RMK Smile Foundation, I am grateful for the chance to brighten lives and open doors of opportunity for children, the aged, and vulnerable communities. Each act of kindness we offer is a small spark that grows into hope, and it is this hope that guides our mission. Together with our supporters, we are building a future where every smile is nurtured, every child is encouraged, and every community feels seen and valued. Thank you
Ruth Marie Kowfie
