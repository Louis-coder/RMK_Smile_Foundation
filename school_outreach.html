RMK SMILE FOUNDATION SCHOOL OUTREACH

We are thrilled to share the success of our recent outreach initiative at Timstimshwe D/A Basic School located in Daboase in the Western Region of Ghana. The school has been facing significant challenges due to limited resources. On 16th May, 2025 RMK SMILE FOUNDATION proudly donated some educational materials to support the students and teachers in their pursuit of knowledge.Our team initially visited the school and engaged with the students and staff to witness their concerns that need immediate attention. Upon our visit to the school, we provided: Books and Learning Materials: Over 400 Exercise Books were shared with the primary students. We provided mathsets, pens, and rulers to the candidates. Other items that were shared included crayons, pencils, erasers sharpeners, and many more. The heartfelt gratitude expressed by the school administration and the enthusiasm of the students reaffirmed the importance of our mission. It was inspiring to see the smiles on their faces as they received the donations, and we are confident that these resources will significantly improve their educational experience.We extend our deepest appreciation to all volunteers and donors who made this outreach possible. To the executives of the foundation, the council members and all members of the foundation, we say a very big thank you for your unwavering support and commitment. Let’s continue to spread smiles and make a lasting impact in the lives of those who need it most. Thank you all.
